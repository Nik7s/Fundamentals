Laptops
- The common battery types found in modern portable devices are Lithium-ion (Li-ion) and Lithium-ion Polymer (LiPo).

- The most common memory module form factor type used in laptops is SODIMM (Small Outline Dual Inline Memory Module).

- The characteristic features of magnetic drives include:
High capacity
Low device cost
Low performance

- The features of Solid-State Drives (SSDs) include:

High performance
Relatively high device cost
Higher capacity in comparison to magnetic drives
